# Source Gap Resolution Playbook

Research date: 2026-06-21

Purpose: convert `recora-ai-citation-analysis` source gaps and source opportunities into implementable page, schema, link, and third-party evidence actions.

## Core Rule

A source gap is not always solvable with owned pages. Some gaps require legitimate third-party evidence, directory/profile correction, review/listing work, partner/customer proof, media coverage, or ongoing monitoring.

## Resolution Matrix

| Classification | When to use | Required evidence | Example source gap | Page action | Third-party action | Expected effect wording | Caveat | Quality-gate trigger |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Owned-page clarification | Existing page has facts but they are vague, buried, or not extractable. | Target page, observed AI answer, missing/unclear claim. | AI answer misunderstands Recora deliverables. | Add direct answer, summary, FAQ, table, caveat, or methodology block. | None by default. | "May improve extraction clarity from the owned page." | Does not guarantee AI use. | Pricing, competitor, customer, methodology claims. |
| New owned page | No owned page answers a recurring prompt-topic or buyer question. | Prompt-topic, page inventory, buyer question, approved facts. | No page explains source gap methodology. | Create glossary, comparison, report, service, pricing, or source intelligence page. | Optional source promotion after publication. | "Creates a crawlable source for a currently missing topic." | New page may not be crawled/indexed/cited. | Claims or comparisons needing evidence. |
| Internal link improvement | Relevant content exists but is isolated or poorly connected. | Link map, page inventory, target topic. | Sample report not linked from LP or pricing page. | Add contextual links with descriptive anchors. | None. | "Supports discovery and page relationship clarity." | Internal links do not guarantee indexing/citation. | Links to private/report-sensitive pages. |
| Structured data support | Visible facts exist and schema can clarify page/entity type. | Visible content, JSON-LD plan, schema/body match. | Product facts visible but no SoftwareApplication schema. | Add body-aligned JSON-LD and validate rendered output. | None. | "Supports machine interpretation of visible facts." | Schema is not proof and does not guarantee ranking/citation. | Review, rating, pricing, offer, customer result fields. |
| Third-party evidence | AI answers cite independent sources and owned page cannot provide independent proof. | Cited source set, competitor source advantage, source type. | Competitor is cited from directory/media while client lacks independent profiles. | Clarify owned facts and link to legitimate public proof where appropriate. | Earn/correct directory, partner, media, or industry profile. | "Addresses the independent evidence side of the source gap." | Must be legitimate; no guaranteed adoption. | Any outreach/review/PR action. |
| Directory/profile correction | Existing third-party profile is missing, stale, or inaccurate. | Profile URL, current text, desired approved facts. | Directory lists wrong product category. | Add owned source-of-truth page if missing. | Request correction through official profile/channel. | "May reduce third-party misinformation." | Third-party site may not update or be used. | Category, pricing, claims, competitor positioning. |
| Review/site listing | Public review/listing surfaces matter for provider answers. | Real reviews/listings, platform policy, approval. | AI answer references review site but client profile is sparse. | Add review/testimonial explanation on owned site if approved. | Encourage legitimate reviews, complete listing, correct facts. | "Improves legitimacy and completeness of public listing evidence." | Never fabricate or incentivize against policy. | Reviews, ratings, testimonials. |
| Media/PR coverage | Public independent coverage is needed for authority or news claims. | Newsworthy fact, approved story, target source policy. | AI cites media for category leaders but client has no coverage. | Publish factual owned explanation/report. | Earn media/PR coverage through legitimate outreach. | "May create independent public evidence for future retrieval." | PR coverage is not guaranteed and must not be manipulative. | Media claims, awards, rankings, funding, results. |
| Partner/customer proof | Claim needs customer/partner validation. | Customer approval, partner page, case study evidence. | AI answer doubts implementation credibility. | Add approved case study or proof block with caveats. | Partner/customer page, quote, case study, or directory listing if approved. | "Adds corroborating evidence where owned claims alone are weak." | Approval and evidence limits apply. | Customer outcome or partner claims. |
| Ongoing monitoring only | Evidence is too weak, volatile, or not actionable yet. | Prompt/provider/date sample, uncertainty reason. | One-off AI answer anomaly with no source pattern. | Do not overbuild pages yet; document hypothesis. | None until pattern repeats. | "Monitor before turning anomaly into implementation work." | Single observations should not drive major work. | Low sample size, missing source text. |
