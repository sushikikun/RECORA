## Common Page Rules

- Keep critical facts in visible text.
- Add schema only after visible/supportable content exists.
- Separate owned-page fixes from third-party evidence work.
- Mark unverified source, competitor, pricing, review, and AI-search claims `NEEDS_VERIFICATION`.
- Include update dates when freshness affects trust.
- Use internal links to move from broad pages to specific proof, definitions, pricing, examples, and reports.
