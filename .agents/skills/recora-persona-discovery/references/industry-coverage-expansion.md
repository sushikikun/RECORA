# Industry Coverage Expansion

Use this reference when the business does not fit the core SaaS, EC, clinic, professional service, school, real estate, recruiting, agency, marketplace, or local-service patterns.

These are routing patterns for persona discovery, not proof of actual customers. Include personas only when site evidence supports the role, trigger, comparison need, and prompt angle.

## Use Rules

- If the site shows broad category language but no industry-specific proof, mark the industry subtype as `weak_hypothesis`.
- If an industry is regulated or high-trust, apply `regulated-industry-cautions.md`.
- If a business has multiple locations, franchisees, or service areas, apply `local-trust-service-personas.md`.
- Do not create personas from industry labels alone. Require a decision role, trigger, comparison axis, evidence need, and prompt angle.
