# GEORADER Product Growth Playbook

Use this reference for GEORADER本体 strategy, product improvement, report packaging, paid diagnosis, and monthly roadmap work.

## Evidence Label

Most product packaging and roadmap recommendations are `GEORADER_ASSUMPTION` unless confirmed by repository files, product docs, or user-provided strategy.

## Product Growth Themes

- move from one-time diagnosis to continuous monitoring and improvement.
- make competitor AI answer gaps tangible.
- connect query evidence to content, technical, authority, and conversion actions.
- make reports useful for internal stakeholder sharing.
- package monthly improvements around measurable re-checks.
- keep sample/provisional states honest.

## Paid Diagnosis Structure

Recommended report sections:

1. scope and evidence.
2. AI visibility summary.
3. non-branded discovery gap.
4. competitor AI/SERP gap.
5. technical discoverability.
6. search intent map.
7. topical/entity authority.
8. citation readiness.
9. conversion SEO and trust risk.
10. P0/P1/P2 roadmap.
11. next 30/60/90 days.

## Monthly Improvement Roadmap

Use:

| Month | Focus | Deliverables | Evidence needed | Re-measurement |
|---|---|---|---|---|

Typical sequence:

- Month 1: fix trust risks, technical blockers, core entity/service clarity.
- Month 2: launch intent and comparison assets.
- Month 3: strengthen proof, citations, case studies, third-party profiles.
- Month 4+: re-run AI answer checks, refresh pages, expand industry/use-case clusters.

## Product Value Checks

- Does the report explain why the client is absent or weak in AI answers?
- Does it show competitors and cited sources without inventing proof?
- Does it separate official guidance, research, industry practice, and GEORADER assumptions?
- Does it lead to a paid action plan rather than generic SEO advice?
- Does it avoid implying automated delivery or diagnosis when provisional?
