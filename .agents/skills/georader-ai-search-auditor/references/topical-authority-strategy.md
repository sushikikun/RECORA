# Topical Authority Strategy

Use this reference for content architecture, topical coverage, and monthly improvement roadmaps.

## Evidence Label

Most topical-authority recommendations are `INDUSTRY_PRACTICE` unless supported by an official source or supplied performance data. GEORADER-specific page priorities are `GEORADER_ASSUMPTION`.

## Core Pattern

Build clusters:

- hub page: defines the topic and links to subtopics.
- spoke pages: answer specific intents, industries, comparisons, FAQs, and proof needs.
- internal links: connect hub, spokes, related cases, and conversion pages.
- proof assets: case studies, sample reports, data, methodology, author/company pages.

## Quality Rules

- Do not recommend thin pages that only swap a keyword or industry name.
- Each page must answer a distinct search intent and support a client decision.
- Use comparison tables when the intent is comparative.
- Use FAQ/glossary formats when the intent is definitional.
- Use case studies and proof pages when the claim needs evidence.
- Use internal links to show relationship among topic, use case, proof, and conversion step.

## Monthly Roadmap Pattern

1. Fix technical blockers and trust risks.
2. Strengthen core service/entity pages.
3. Add comparison and problem-aware pages.
4. Add industry/use-case pages with real proof.
5. Refresh pages based on AI answer and SERP results.
6. Expand third-party proof and citation sources.
