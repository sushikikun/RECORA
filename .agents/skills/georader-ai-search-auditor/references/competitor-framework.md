# Competitor Framework

Use this reference for competitor SERP / AI answer gap audits and strategy.

## Competitor Classes

Compare against direct and indirect alternatives:

- SEO agencies.
- Web marketing agencies.
- PR agencies.
- brand monitoring tools.
- AI search optimization services.
- overseas GEO / AIO / LLMO tools as reference cases.
- manual ChatGPT self-checking as an alternative behavior.
- internal marketing teams doing ad hoc checks.
- content platforms, review sites, directories, and media pages that AI systems may cite instead of brands.

## Comparison Dimensions

For each competitor or class, evaluate:

- search visibility and SERP presence when evidence is supplied.
- AI answer visibility.
- mention position and frequency.
- recommendation strength.
- cited sources and cited domains.
- positioning clarity.
- topical coverage.
- entity/brand proof.
- pricing/package clarity.
- target specificity.
- report quality.
- ongoing support.
- agency resale suitability.
- conversion trust and delivery clarity.

## Rules

- Do not invent competitor SERP or AI answer performance without evidence.
- If competitors are unknown, propose competitor classes and ask for names only when exact brands are required.
- Distinguish "competitor mentioned by AI", "competitor ranking in SERP", and "business competitor selected by the user".
- Include indirect alternatives because AI answers often recommend broader agencies, directories, review sites, or manual workflows instead of dedicated GEO tools.
- Treat competitor strategy recommendations as `INDUSTRY_PRACTICE` or `GEORADER_ASSUMPTION` unless backed by supplied evidence.
