# Expert Diagnosis Decision Tree

Use this reference when diagnosing why a brand may not appear, may not be cited, may not be recommended, or may lose to competitors in SEO, GEO, or AI search.

Do not invent answer data, SERP results, crawler behavior, competitors, citations, or business outcomes. If evidence is missing, mark the branch `NEEDS_VERIFICATION`.

## A. Brand Absent From AI Answers

| Diagnostic question | Evidence needed | Likely severity | Recommended next action | Recora report section |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Was the query set designed around real non-branded, problem-aware, comparison, and buyer-intent prompts? | Query list, target ICP, category, buyer journey, sales objections | P1, or P0 if the report already claims visibility | Rebuild the query set before diagnosing absence | Search intent gaps |
| Is actual answer data missing? | AI answer exports with query, date, model/source, citations, and prompt text | P0 for report validity | Request or run fresh AI answer evidence before claiming absence | Evidence scope |
| Is the brand clearly associated with the category and problem? | Homepage, service pages, metadata, about page, entity descriptions | P1 | Clarify service category, target customer, and use cases in crawlable text | Entity / brand authority |
| Is the service description specific enough for an AI answer to summarize? | Service pages, pricing/process pages, docs, sales materials | P1 | Add concise service, target, method, and outcome explanations | SEO foundation / citation readiness |
| Are important pages crawlable and indexable? | robots.txt, sitemap, status codes, meta robots, canonical, rendered HTML | P0 if critical pages are blocked, otherwise P1 | Verify technical access and plan blocker fixes | SEO foundation |
| Is important content hidden behind JavaScript, forms, gated PDFs, or images? | Rendered page, HTML, screenshots, crawl output | P1 | Make key claims and proof visible in accessible text | SEO foundation / AI citation readiness |
| Are non-branded intent pages missing? | Content inventory, query map, competitor pages | P1 | Create or improve problem-aware, comparison, alternative, and industry pages | Search intent gaps |
| Do competitors have stronger comparison, proof, or decision assets? | Competitor pages, supplied SERP/AI answer evidence | P1 | Build better comparison, proof, methodology, and case-study assets | Competitor SERP / AI gap |
| Are external mentions weak or absent? | Third-party profiles, reviews, directories, press, partner pages | P1 or P2 depending on market | Strengthen accurate third-party proof and entity consistency | Entity / brand authority |
| Are brand signals inconsistent? | About page, schema, social/profile pages, directories, docs | P1 | Clean up naming, category, descriptions, and official facts | Entity / brand authority |
| Does the AI answer source set favor directories, media, or competitors? | Cited domains from supplied AI answers | P1 | Decide whether to improve owned pages, earn third-party mentions, or both | Competitor SERP / AI gap |

## B. Brand Mentioned But Not Recommended

| Diagnostic question | Evidence needed | Likely severity | Recommended next action | Recora report section |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Does the answer mention the brand without a clear reason to choose it? | Supplied AI answer text and cited sources | P1 | Add differentiated positioning and decision criteria | AI search visibility |
| Is differentiation weak? | Product pages, comparison pages, sales docs, competitor pages | P1 | Clarify what the brand is best for and what it is not best for | Competitor SERP / AI gap |
| Is the target customer unclear? | Service pages, pricing, use cases, testimonials | P1 | State ICP, company size, use case, and exclusions | Entity / brand authority |
| Are pricing, use cases, or process unclear? | Pricing page, process page, FAQ, sales docs | P1 | Add pricing range, process, implementation scope, and buyer FAQ where appropriate | Conversion SEO |
| Are case studies weak or missing? | Case-study pages, proof assets, client quotes | P1 | Add specific proof, outcomes, constraints, and examples | AI citation readiness |
| Do competitor pages explain decision criteria better? | Competitor comparison pages and answer citations | P1 | Build comparison assets that answer selection criteria fairly | Competitor SERP / AI gap |
| Is third-party validation missing? | Reviews, directories, partner pages, press, community mentions | P1 or P2 | Improve accurate external validation without fabricating proof | Entity / brand authority |
| Do conversion assets reduce buyer risk? | CTAs, demo flow, sample reports, security/process FAQ | P1 | Add risk-reduction assets: sample report, checklist, process, proof, FAQ | Conversion SEO |

## C. Brand Cited Incorrectly Or Hallucinated

| Diagnostic question | Evidence needed | Likely severity | Recommended next action | Recora report section |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Is public information outdated? | Cited pages, cached snippets, old profiles, directories | P0 if client-harming, otherwise P1 | Identify incorrect sources and update owned and third-party facts | Trust risk / entity authority |
| Are service descriptions inconsistent across owned sources? | Homepage, product pages, docs, about page, schema | P1 | Standardize official definitions and service naming | Entity / brand authority |
| Is there an official definition page? | Brand glossary, about, methodology, product overview | P1 | Publish or improve an authoritative brand/category definition page | AI citation readiness |
| Are company/entity pages weak? | About, contact, author/operator, schema, profiles | P1 | Strengthen official entity pages and consistency | Entity / brand authority |
| Do third-party sources contain incorrect information? | Directory, review, partner, press pages | P1 | Prioritize correction or replacement of high-visibility inaccurate sources | Competitor SERP / AI gap |
| Does the AI answer lack reliable citations? | Supplied answer with citations or no citations | P0 for report accuracy | Mark hallucination risk and avoid treating the answer as fact | Evidence scope |

## D. SEO Visible But AI Answer Invisible

| Diagnostic question | Evidence needed | Likely severity | Recommended next action | Recora report section |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Does the page rank organically but fail to answer decision criteria? | SERP evidence, page content, AI answer output | P1 | Add buyer decision sections, comparisons, use cases, and proof | Search intent gaps |
| Is content extractable as standalone evidence? | Page text, headings, tables, FAQs, schema opportunity | P1 | Add clear summaries, definitions, FAQ, comparison, and methodology blocks | AI citation readiness |
| Does the page answer a keyword but not the user's real job-to-be-done? | Query map, sales objections, page copy | P1 | Reframe content around problem, use case, and selection criteria | Topical authority gaps |
| Are citations or proof weak? | Case studies, metrics, source-backed claims, third-party mentions | P1 | Add proof assets and clarify provenance | AI citation readiness |
| Are competitors easier to summarize? | Competitor pages and AI answer snippets | P1 | Improve concise positioning and structured decision support | Competitor SERP / AI gap |

## E. AI Answer Visible But Conversion Weak

| Diagnostic question | Evidence needed | Likely severity | Recommended next action | Recora report section |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Does the landing page CTA match AI-answer intent? | AI answer query, landing page, CTA, funnel path | P1 | Align CTA with visitor stage: compare, diagnose, demo, sample, or consult | Conversion SEO |
| Is there a sample report or concrete output? | Sample report, screenshots, product proof | P1 | Add sample report or output preview with honest sample labeling | Conversion SEO |
| Are pricing and process clear enough? | Pricing/process page, FAQ, sales docs | P1 | Clarify package, timeline, next steps, and what the user receives | Conversion SEO |
| Is trust proof missing? | Testimonials, case studies, logos, security/process proof | P1 | Add credible proof and avoid fabricated metrics | Entity / brand authority |
| Is form or delivery flow unclear? | Form copy, confirmation copy, backend evidence if supplied | P0 when completion is implied without evidence | Make provisional states honest and separate implementation planning | Trust risk |
| Do claims feel exaggerated? | Page/report copy | P0 if false official or guaranteed claims, otherwise P1 | Replace guarantee language with evidence-scoped wording | Trust risk / report quality |

## Diagnosis Output Pattern

Use:

| Hypothesis | Evidence found | Evidence missing | Severity | Next action | Report section |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|

If more than one cause is plausible, rank hypotheses by:

1. client trust risk.
2. measurement validity.
3. technical access.
4. brand/category clarity.
5. proof and citation readiness.
6. competitor decision support.
7. conversion impact.
