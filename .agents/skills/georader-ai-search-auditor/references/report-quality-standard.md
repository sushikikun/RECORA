# Report Quality Standard

Use this reference for client-facing reports, sample reports, report templates, and report QA.

## Quality Bar

A strong GEORADER report should:

- state the target, date, evidence scope, and limitations.
- separate confirmed facts, official guidance, research-backed findings, industry practice, GEORADER assumptions, and needs-verification items when relevant.
- show AI visibility and SEO/GEO gaps in a way a client can understand.
- explain competitor gaps without exaggeration.
- show citations and missing evidence.
- include technical discoverability, intent coverage, topical/entity authority, citation readiness, and conversion SEO where relevant.
- rank findings by P0/P1/P2.
- connect findings to business impact.
- include specific next actions.
- avoid unsupported claims of completed diagnosis, delivery, payment, automation, ranking, or AI citation.

## Internal Output

Internal output may include:

- blunt severity notes.
- likely implementation areas.
- test ideas.
- report pipeline concerns.
- product packaging opportunities.
- paid diagnosis and monthly roadmap opportunities.
- sales implications.

## Client-Facing Output

Client-facing output must:

- be specific but not alarmist.
- avoid blame.
- avoid implementation details that distract from business value.
- avoid exposing internal uncertainty except as clear scope limits.
- use plain Japanese for Japanese clients.
- explain GEORADER's review framework as internal, not official.
- include a practical next-action section.

## Client Report Skeleton

Use when helpful:

1. 監査対象と確認範囲.
2. 主要な発見.
3. AI検索での見え方.
4. 競合との差分.
5. 技術・クロール・インデックス面の確認.
6. 検索意図とコンテンツギャップ.
7. 引用・根拠として使える情報の不足.
8. CVにつながる改善余地.
9. P0/P1/P2改善ロードマップ.
10. 次に確認すること.

## Report QA Checklist

- Is every score traceable to evidence?
- Are official claims actually backed by official sources?
- Are competitors named only when evidence supports them?
- Are citations separated from uncited claims?
- Are screenshots, SERPs, or AI outputs represented accurately?
- Are provisional or unverified flows labeled honestly?
- Can sales use the report without promising unsupported delivery, ranking, or AI citation?
